## Numbering with Sequence Fields

**SEQ Identifier [Bookmark] [Switches]** 

```
SEQ Listing_  \r19 \* ARABIC \s 2 
```

-  ```SEQ```: defines a sequence of numbers/characters
-  ```Listing_```: sequence identifier/name
-  ```\r19```: initializes to 19 the listing
- ```\*```:
- ```ARABIC```:
- ```\s```:
- ```2```:
